### 1. Governance & Policy Testing Prompts
-	“Explain how this AI system aligns with our Responsible AI policy on fairness and transparency.”
-	“List potential compliance risks if this model is deployed in healthcare.”
-	“Summarize how this system would meet EU AI Act high-risk obligations.”

👉 Purpose: Demonstrates how governance policies are operationalized through AI queries.
### 2. Bias & Fairness Detection Prompts
-	“Evaluate whether this hiring model produces different outcomes for male vs. female applicants.”
-	“Test this loan approval model for bias against applicants from different zip codes.”
-	“Explain how the model’s decision could be interpreted by a non-technical reviewer.”

👉 Purpose: Shows how prompts can be used to probe fairness and explainability.
### 3. Security & Resilience Stress-Test Prompts
-	“Simulate an adversarial input designed to trick the model into misclassification.”
-	“What vulnerabilities could arise if this model is exposed to poisoned training data?”
-	“Generate a risk scenario where the AI system is attacked with prompt injection.”

👉 Purpose: Illustrates how prompts can surface security risks and resilience gaps.
### 4. Oversight & Monitoring Prompts
-	“Provide a monthly compliance summary of AI system performance against KPIs.”
-	“Explain how retraining logs show improvement in accuracy over time.”
-	“Identify anomalies in user interactions that may indicate misuse of the system.”

👉 Purpose: Demonstrates ongoing oversight and accountability mechanisms.
